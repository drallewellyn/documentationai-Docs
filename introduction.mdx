## Important — what we are and aren't

AdvanceMed provides **general career information and coaching** for medical professionals. We are not the registration body, the migration regulator, or a clinical training provider. Specifically:

- We provide **general** career information, not individual financial, legal, or clinical advice.
- We **cannot give migration advice** — only Registered Migration Agents and Migration Lawyers can. See [Visa types relevant to doctors](/help-center/international-medical-graduates/visa-types).
- AHPRA / Medical Board of Australia / specialist colleges remain authoritative for registration and training questions.

For the legal terms governing your use of our services, see [Terms](https://advancemed.com.au/terms/) and [Privacy Policy](https://advancemed.com.au/privacy/).
