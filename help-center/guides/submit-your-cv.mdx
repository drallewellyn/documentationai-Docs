For an overview of the rewrite service itself, see [How does the CV / Resume rewrite work?](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite).

## The flow at a glance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase via coaching.advancemed.com.au" icon="shopping-bag">
    Sign in (or create an account) at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au) and purchase the **early-career** or **established-career** version of the CV / Resume rewrite from the shop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the intake form" icon="edit-3">
    All AdvanceMed document service intake forms live at [coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms/).

    Pick the form for the service you purchased. You'll receive an **email reminder** with a direct link to the right form, but you can always navigate there manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your current CV" icon="upload">
    Attach your current CV (PDF or Word, both fine for intake — final delivery is PDF). If you don't have a current CV, upload a LinkedIn export or any work-history document you have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit" icon="send">
    Once submitted, AdvanceMed will return a first draft within **5 working days**, with a video walking you through it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to log in

Your customer portal is at:

[coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms/)

If you're a VIP member, you also have access via the VIP portal — see [VIP portal](/help-center/services/vip-portal).

## What to have ready before you fill in the form

- Your **current CV** in any format
- A list of **target roles or employers** if you have one
- Your **post-nominals** and registration details
- Any **selection criteria** you've been asked to address (these inform tailoring)
- Recent achievements you'd like emphasised — papers, awards, leadership roles, audits

## What happens next

See [How does the CV / Resume rewrite work?](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite) for the rest of the flow — feedback, revisions, final delivery, and the included VisualCV licence.

## Common issues

**I can't find the intake form.** Go to [coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms/) directly and pick the form matching the service you purchased.

**I uploaded the wrong file.** Reply to the form-confirmation email with the correct file attached. We'll swap it in before starting.

**I haven't received the email reminder.** Check spam, then proceed via the forms page directly — the reminder is a convenience, not a gate.
