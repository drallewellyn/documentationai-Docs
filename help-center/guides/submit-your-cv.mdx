For an overview of the rewrite service, see [How does the CV / Resume rewrite work?](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite).

## Step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your option" icon="list">
    Visit [my.advancemed.com.au/cv-and-resume-options](https://my.advancemed.com.au/cv-and-resume-options/) and select either the **early-career** or **established-career** version of the rewrite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay" icon="credit-card">
    Complete checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to the intake form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the intake form" icon="edit-3">
    Click the intake form link in the confirmation email. The form asks for career details, target roles, and any specific feedback you've had on past applications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your current CV" icon="upload">
    Attach your current CV (PDF or Word, both fine for upload — the final delivery will still be PDF). If you don't have a current CV, upload a LinkedIn export or any work-history document you have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit" icon="send">
    Submit the form. AdvanceMed will return a first draft within **5 working days**, with a video walking you through it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to have ready before you start

- Your **current CV** in any format
- A list of **target roles or employers** if you have one
- Your **post-nominals** and registration details
- Any **selection criteria** you've been asked to address (these inform tailoring)
- Recent achievements you'd like emphasised — papers, awards, leadership roles, audits

## What happens next

See [How does the CV / Resume rewrite work?](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite) for the rest of the flow — feedback, revisions, final delivery, and the included VisualCV licence.

## Common issues

**The intake link doesn't work.** Check that it's the link from the *purchase confirmation email*, not from a marketing email. If still stuck, email **support@advancemed.com.au** with your order ID.

**I uploaded the wrong file.** Reply to the confirmation email with the correct file attached. We'll swap it in before starting.
