Most of Dr Anthony Llewellyn's clients find this a straightforward process to set up. There are three steps.

## Step 1 — Book and pay

Go to [coaching.advancemed.com.au/strategy-call](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/strategy-call/) and click **BOOK NOW**.

Fill in your details (email, name, address) and pay for your coaching session online.

## Step 2 — Complete the pre-call survey

You'll be taken to a thank-you page and directed to a coaching client's page where you can fill in a pre-call survey to get the most out of your call.

## Step 3 — Pick a time

Click through to the booking page and find a date and time that suits you. **Please book only one appointment.**

Booking link: [tidycal.com/advancemed/strategy-call-prepaid](https://tidycal.com/advancemed/strategy-call-prepaid)

From this link you can also book future sessions, or rebook / reschedule if you need to.

## Before your session

You'll receive an email with the appointment and a text reminder a day out.

If you haven't used Zoom before, spend a few minutes setting it up on your computer beforehand. On the day of your appointment, use the link sent to log in a few minutes before it starts.

All sessions are recorded for you to review afterwards.
