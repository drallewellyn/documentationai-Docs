A **coaching series** is multi-session 1-on-1 work with Dr Anthony Llewellyn — different from a one-off [Strategy or Clarity Call](/help-center/services/strategy-clarity-calls), which is a single 30-minute session.

## When a series is the right fit

You're a good candidate for a coaching series if you have a **multi-step career goal** that won't be solved in one call. Common examples:

- Preparing for a specialist training application or interview cycle
- Navigating the IMG registration pathway end-to-end
- A career pivot into or out of a specialty
- Preparing a job search across multiple roles or jurisdictions
- Supporting an unsuccessful interview cycle and resetting for the next round

If your question is "should I do X?" or "how does Y work?", a single Clarity Call usually beats a series.

## Format

- Sessions are **45+ minutes**, conducted online over Zoom
- Cadence is agreed at the start — typically weekly, fortnightly, or aligned to milestones in your application cycle
- All sessions are recorded — see [Session recordings](/help-center/account-and-billing/session-recordings)
- Between sessions you'll usually have **prep work** (reflection, drafts, research) — most of the value sits between sessions, not inside them

## What gets covered (varies by client)

| Phase | Typical focus |
|---|---|
| Discovery | Career goals, strengths, gaps, time horizon |
| Strategy | Target roles, target employers, registration path, timeline |
| Application | CV / resume, cover letters, selection criteria, networking |
| Interview prep | Mock questions, panel dynamics, post-mortems |
| Decision support | Offers, negotiation, transitions |

## What's not in scope

- **Migration / visa advice** — only Registered Migration Agents and Migration Lawyers can give that. See [Visa types relevant to doctors](/help-center/international-medical-graduates/visa-types).
- **Clinical or specialty-specific clinical advice** — coaching is about career, not clinical practice.
- **Financial or legal advice** — general information only.

## How to engage

1. Start with a [Clarity Call](/help-center/services/strategy-clarity-calls) so we can scope the right package.
2. After that call, Anthony will recommend a series length (typically 4–12 weeks) based on what you need.
3. Document services (CV rewrite, cover letters, selection criteria) can be folded in as add-ons.

## Refunds and cancellation

See [Refund and cancellation policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation). Individual sessions in a series follow the **24-hour cancellation rule**.
