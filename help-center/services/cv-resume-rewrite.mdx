Our CV / Resume rewrite service produces a professional-looking doctor's resume that's more likely to grab the attention of employers.

You can purchase the service via your customer dashboard at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au) — sign in or create an account, then look for the CV / Resume options in the shop.

## The process

1. Select either the **early-career** or **established-career** version of the service at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au).
2. Make payment.
3. Fill in the intake form at [coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms/) and upload a current version of your CV / resume. You'll receive an email reminder with a direct link.

We then provide a first draft of your new resume (normally within **5 working days**), along with a short video walking you through it and highlighting any areas needing your input.

You provide feedback. We return a final version normally within **8 working days**.

About 95% of customers don't need it, but there is an option for a half-hour video call to clarify aspects of your resume.

## Important notes

Our ability to deliver on time depends on your promptness and the clarity of the information you provide about your career. One common problem we identify is a lack of explicit achievements in candidates' work, teaching, education and other relevant history.

Our service does **not** extend to:

- making up stories for your resume
- spending excessive time reviewing other documents you might submit
- spending excessive time interviewing you to obtain the correct information

The resume is provided in **PDF only** — see [Can I have a Word version of my CV?](/help-center/services/word-version-of-cv) for alternatives.

We are normally generous with edits and fine-tuning, but we have a policy of **no more than 2 revisions**.

## Items that incur further cost

- Cover letters in addition to the resume
- More than one version, or different versions, of a resume
- Excessive interview time to gather information
- Updating your resume at a later point in time
