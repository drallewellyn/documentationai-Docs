AdvanceMed provides **3 free courses for International Medical Graduates** that step you through the main 3 pathways to working as a doctor in Australia.

## The courses

- [Specialist Pathway Course](https://my.advancemed.com.au/course/specialist-pathway-course/)
- [Standard Pathway Course](https://my.advancemed.com.au/course/becoming-a-doctor-in-australia/)
- [Competent Authority Pathway Course](https://my.advancemed.com.au/course/competent-authority-pathway/)

## After the course
