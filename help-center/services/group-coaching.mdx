## Why group coaching can work

When everyone in the room is preparing for the **same kind of interview**, **same application cycle**, or **same registration pathway**, the discussion gets denser fast. You learn from other candidates' questions, see how panels would view different responses, and gain practice you couldn't get in 1-on-1 sessions alone.

## When 1-on-1 still wins

- Your situation is unusual
- You've had setbacks you want to talk through privately
- You need detailed CV / cover letter / selection-criteria help

For most doctors, the right answer is a mix: a [Clarity Call](/help-center/services/strategy-clarity-calls) first to scope, then either a 1-on-1 [coaching series](/help-center/services/coaching-series) or, if a relevant cohort is running, group coaching.

## Possible cohort programs (sample formats)

| Cohort | Focus | Format |
|---|---|---|
| RANZCP application coaching | Stage 1 / Stage 2 applicants | 4–6 weekly group sessions + 1 individual review |
| Surgical training prep | SET selection cohorts | Cohort of 6–10, weekly for the application window |
| IMG specialist pathway | Cohort of IMGs in active comparability | Monthly group calls + individual touchpoints |
| Senior leadership transition | Consultants moving into Director / HoD roles | 6 fortnightly sessions |

These are examples — actual cohorts run only when there's enough demand. Email **support@advancemed.com.au** to register interest.

## Refunds and cancellations

Group programs are typically priced per cohort and refunds follow the standard policy plus the cohort's specific terms (which will be sent to you before you commit). See [Refund and cancellation policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation).
