When you purchase a [CV / Resume rewrite](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite), the price includes a **12-month VisualCV licence**. VisualCV is the third-party CV-builder platform AdvanceMed uses to design and deliver doctor CVs.

## What's included

- Access to your finished CV in the VisualCV editor
- Ability to **edit text** (work history, achievements, dates) yourself
- **Export to PDF** any time — keeps the same professional layout
- Version control — keep multiple drafts (e.g. tailored versions for different roles)
- Cover letter and personal-statement templates

## What's not included

- Word-format export — VisualCV produces PDFs only. See [Can I have a Word version?](/help-center/services/word-version-of-cv) for alternatives.
- **Layout changes** are limited by the template chosen during your rewrite. If you want a different layout you'll generally need to start over.
- Re-design or major restructure — that's a fresh CV rewrite engagement.

## Why a VisualCV licence rather than just a finished file

CVs need updating. Once a year you'll add a new role, a publication, a course. Doing that in VisualCV takes minutes; doing it in Word usually breaks formatting. The licence is meant to keep you editing your own CV cheaply over time, rather than paying us to do small updates.

## Extending past 12 months

Once your 12-month included licence runs out, you have two options:

1. **Continue your VisualCV subscription directly** with VisualCV — recommended. Subscription pricing is on the VisualCV site and is independent of AdvanceMed.
2. **Download a final PDF before the licence expires**, archive it, and re-engage AdvanceMed when you next need a substantial update.

## Common questions

**Can I share my VisualCV account with a colleague?**
No — the licence is for the named client only.

**Can I migrate the design to a different platform?**
The visual design is VisualCV's; you can copy your text content out to anywhere, but the look-and-feel won't migrate.

**My licence has lapsed and I can't access my CV.**
Email **support@advancemed.com.au** — we may be able to retrieve a final PDF on request, but please don't rely on this as a backup strategy.

## Need a major update?

If your career has moved substantially since your last rewrite (new role, new specialty, new pathway), it's usually faster and cheaper to engage a fresh rewrite than to retrofit. See [How does the CV / Resume rewrite work?](/help-center/services/cv-resume-rewrite).
