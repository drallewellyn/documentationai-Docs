AdvanceMed's **VIP programs** are paid coaching packages combining 1-on-1 sessions, course access, AI tools, and the VIP community at [doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au). Three tiers — pick the one that matches your situation and budget.

## What every VIP tier includes

- **VIP portal access** at [doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au) (via the UpCoach platform)
- **1 Strategy Call** with your coach
- **Lifetime access to all paid courses** — Interview Skills, Cover Letters & Applications, Medical CV, Expert Series
- **AI tools library** — Personal Statement Builder, Interview Questions Builder, and more
- **eGuides library** — 12+ guides covering templates, CV design, and preparation checklists
- **VIP forums** for resource sharing with other VIP members
- **Ongoing email and chat support** for the duration of your program
- **VisualCV builder** subscription with complimentary CV reviews

## The three tiers

| Tier | 1-on-1 time | Price (AUD upfront) | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **General VIP** | Strategy Call + 6 sessions | **$5,497** | Doctors wanting comprehensive career support over months — application cycles, training pathways, leadership transitions |
| **SET VIP** | Strategy Call + 6 sessions | **$5,497** | Doctors applying for RACS SET (Surgical Education Training) selection — coached by Dr Melody Koo |
| **Mini VIP** | Strategy Call + 2 sessions | **$2,997** | Shorter engagements — 2–3 weeks to prepare for a specific milestone (e.g. an interview round) |

Coaching sessions run **45+ minutes** and each counts as one hour of allocated time. Note: a CV rewrite typically uses 2 sessions; a cover letter review uses 1.

Stated values: General and SET have **$9,200+** value; Mini has **$5,100+** value (at standard non-VIP rates).

## Payment options

All three tiers offer:

- **Upfront** payment in full, **or**
- **Installments** via SureCart at checkout

Prices are quoted in AUD. Checkout supports multiple currencies (AUD, GBP, CAD, CNY, EUR, INR, NZD, USD).

## Checkout bump — extra 2 sessions

At checkout for **General VIP** and **SET VIP**, you can choose to add **another 2 coaching sessions** as a checkout bump for an additional fee. This is the easiest way to extend your 1-on-1 allocation. The bump is **not** available for Mini VIP.

## Availability

VIP programs run in cohorts with limited slots:

- **General VIP** — typically sold out; new slots open periodically
- **SET VIP** — limited annual availability tied to the RACS SET selection cycle (currently **sold out for 2026**)
- **Mini VIP** — most consistently available

If your preferred tier is sold out, register interest with **support@advancemed.com.au** and we'll let you know when slots open.

## Choosing the right tier

| If you… | Start with |
|---|---|

- You'll receive login details for [doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au).
- The same `support@advancemed.com.au` inbox handles VIP support — there is no separate VIP-only support channel.
- Forgotten your password? See [I can't reset my password](/help-center/troubleshooting/cannot-reset-password) for the VIP-portal-specific reset URL.
- Book your Strategy Call and individual sessions via the coaching link inside the VIP portal.

## VIP vs one-off purchases

| Question | One-off (Strategy Call, CV rewrite, individual course) | VIP membership |

| Lifetime course access? | No — per-purchase | Yes — all current and future paid courses |

## Cancellation and refunds

VIP purchases follow the standard [Refund and cancellation policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation), including the **14-day refund window** applying to the initial purchase. Installment plans must be paid through to the agreed schedule once the refund window closes.

Individual coaching sessions inside a VIP program still follow the **24-hour cancellation rule** — see [How to reschedule or rebook](/help-center/account-and-billing/reschedule-or-rebook).
