AdvanceMed's **VIP programs** are membership tiers giving ongoing access to coaching content, support forums, and tools at [doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au).

## What VIP members get

All VIP tiers include:

- **Support forums** — discuss your career challenges with other VIP members
- **Coaching links** — book and access included coaching sessions
- **Courses** — VIP-only and tier-specific content
- **AI tools** — career tools available only to VIP members

### General VIP

The flagship tier. For doctors who want ongoing access to AdvanceMed coaching content, AI tools, and the support community across the year.

Includes Dr Anthony Llewellyn's full coaching content library.

### SET VIP

For doctors preparing for **Surgical Education Training (SET)** selection — built around Dr Melody Koo's surgical career coaching content.

Best fit if you're applying for surgical training (RACS SET program) or sitting in surgical sub-specialty selection processes.

### Mini VIP

A lower-commitment entry-tier of the VIP program, with a curated subset of the VIP content.

## Both Anthony's and Melody's programs

The VIP umbrella covers programs delivered by both Dr Anthony Llewellyn and Dr Melody Koo:

- **Anthony Llewellyn** — Career Doctor; psychiatry consultant; broad medical career coaching
- **Melody Koo** — Vascular Surgery Registrar; Surgical Training Coach

When you join VIP, you get access to relevant content from both, depending on your tier.

## VIP vs one-off purchases

| Question | One-off (Strategy Call, CV rewrite, individual course) | VIP membership |
|---|---|---|
| Where do I log in? | `coaching.advancemed.com.au` | `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au` |
| Ongoing access to content? | No — per-purchase | Yes, for the membership term |
| Coaching included? | Per session purchased | Included as part of tier |
| Support forum access? | No | Yes |
| AI tools? | No | Yes |
| Best for | Specific one-off questions, single deliverables | Ongoing career support across months |

## Cancellation and refunds

VIP memberships follow the standard [Refund and cancellation policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation), with the **14-day refund window** applying to the initial purchase.

Annual or recurring tiers must be cancelled before the next renewal — see [How to cancel or change your subscription](/help-center/account-and-billing/cancel-or-change-subscription).
