All prices are in **Australian Dollars** and may be subject to GST. Pricing on this page is indicative — the exact amount payable at checkout always takes precedence.

## At a glance

| Service | Format | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy / Clarity Call | 30 min, online | One focused career session with Dr Anthony Llewellyn, recording included |
| Coaching series | 45+ min sessions, multi-week | Ongoing 1-on-1 support; cadence and total sessions agreed up front |
| CV / Resume rewrite (early career) | Project-based | Drafted resume + walkthrough video, up to 2 revisions, PDF delivery |
| CV / Resume rewrite (established career) | Project-based | As above, scoped for senior / consultant CVs |
| Cover letter / selection criteria | Add-on | Available **after** a CV rewrite or coaching engagement only |
| Document amendments | Per hour | Updates to a previously-delivered document |
| Online courses | Self-paced | Includes the three free IMG pathway courses; paid courses listed separately |
| VIP programs | Cohort coaching package | See breakdown below — three tiers from $2,997 to $5,497 AUD |

## What's not included by default

- Cover letters or selection-criteria responses (purchase separately, after a CV rewrite or coaching engagement)
- Word-format CV files (PDF only — see [Can I have a Word version of my CV?](/help-center/services/word-version-of-cv))
- Multiple resume variants for different roles (additional fee per variant)
- Future updates to your CV after the initial delivery (per-hour rate)

## Refunds

See [Refund and cancellation policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation) for the full policy. Headline rules:

- **Sessions** — at least **24 hours' notice** to cancel or reschedule
- **Online courses or Clarity Calls** — refunds available within **14 days** of purchase
- **Events** — at least **30 days' notice** to cancel
- A **10% administration fee** may apply to discretionary refunds

## GST

Unless otherwise indicated, amounts on the website do not include GST. GST is added at checkout where it applies.
