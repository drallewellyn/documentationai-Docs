We're happy to help with cover letters and selection criteria.

## Our policy

Dr Anthony Llewellyn only offers cover-letter and selection-criteria work to clients who have **first engaged for a CV rewrite or a coaching series**.

It's impossible to write meaningful cover letters or selection criteria without first knowing your career. Unlike some providers, we don't make up selection-criteria responses.

## How to engage

Cover letters and selection criteria are typically done **alongside a review of your CV or resume**, although we can assist with them as a standalone project for existing clients.

Head to our [Document Services page](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/document-services) to review options and pricing.
