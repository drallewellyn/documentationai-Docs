All Strategy Calls, Clarity Calls, and coaching sessions are recorded — primarily so you can review them after the call rather than trying to take notes during.

## How recordings are delivered

After your session ends:

1. The recording is processed (this can take from minutes to a few hours, depending on length).
2. You'll receive an **email with a link** to access the recording.
3. The link goes to your customer dashboard at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au), under **My Recordings**.

## Format

- Video and audio recording (MP4)
- Includes any screen-share or document review portions
- Streamable in the dashboard; usually downloadable as well

## Privacy of recordings

- Only **you** and the AdvanceMed team can access your recording.
- Recordings are not shared, sold, or used for marketing without your written consent.
- See [Privacy and how we handle your data](/help-center/account-and-billing/privacy-and-data) for the full policy.

## How long recordings are kept

Recordings remain available in your dashboard for **as long as your account is active**. We recommend downloading a copy if you want to keep it permanently.

If you cancel your account, contact support before doing so to retrieve any recordings you'd like to keep.

## Didn't get a recording email?

- Check **spam / junk** first.
- Confirm the email on file matches your booking email.
- Email **support@advancemed.com.au** with your session date and registered email.
