For subscription clients, you can manage your credit-card information by logging into your account and visiting the **customer dashboard**:

[coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard/?tab=dashboard)

The platform allows you to add or remove card details as needed.

## Security

We do **not** store your full credit-card details on this site. Card data is handled by **Stripe**, ensuring your payment information is processed securely through a PCI-compliant third-party payment processor rather than stored locally.
