This page is a plain-English summary. The legally-binding document is the full Privacy Policy at [advancemed.com.au/privacy](https://advancemed.com.au/privacy/).

AdvanceMed is a registered trading name of **Great Mynds Pty Ltd** (ABN 13 613 634 343), based in Newcastle, NSW, Australia.

## What we collect

When you buy a service or use the website, AdvanceMed collects:

- Your **name, email, billing address, phone number** for orders
- **Payment details** — handled directly by our payment providers (Stripe, PayPal); we don't store full card numbers
- **Browsing data** — pages you visit, products you view, IP address, browser type
- **Comments or content** you post on the site, including the comment-form data and IP address (used for spam detection)

## Who processes your data

| Provider | What they do |
|---|---|
| **Stripe** and **PayPal** | Process payments for orders and subscriptions |
| **Xero** | Accounting and tax records |
| **Rasa.io** | Newsletter delivery (only if you've subscribed) |
| **Google** | Site analytics |
| **Just Hockey** | Analytics |
| **GetGist** | Site chatbot |

These providers receive only the data they need to do their job.

## How long we keep it

| Data type | Retention |
|---|---|
| Order and tax records | **7 years** (legal requirement) |
| User account data | Until you ask for deletion |
| Comments | Indefinitely |
| Login cookies | 2 days, or 2 weeks if "Remember Me" |

## Your rights

You can ask AdvanceMed to:

- **Send you a copy** of the personal data held about you
- **Delete** your personal data (excluding records that must be retained for tax, legal, or security reasons)
- **Correct** any data that is wrong or out of date

To make a request, email the privacy contact listed at [advancemed.com.au/privacy](https://advancemed.com.au/privacy/#contact).

## Cookies

AdvanceMed uses cookies for login state, screen preferences, and comment form pre-fill. You can clear or block cookies at any time in your browser — but you may need to log in more often.

## Sharing with third parties

AdvanceMed does not sell your data and only shares it with third parties **with your express permission**, except where the third party is providing one of the services listed above (e.g. processing your payment).

## Health information

CVs, application materials, and coaching session notes can include sensitive personal or career information. AdvanceMed treats these as confidential. They are not shared outside the AdvanceMed team without your explicit instruction.
