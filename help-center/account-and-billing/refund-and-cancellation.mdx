This page summarises AdvanceMed's refund and cancellation policy for everyday use. It is not the legal document. The full Terms and Conditions take precedence in any dispute: [advancemed.com.au/terms](https://advancemed.com.au/terms/).

<Callout kind="info">
  Australian Consumer Law remedies (refund, replacement or repair where there's a failure with goods or services) apply regardless of anything on this page.
</Callout>

## Sessions (Strategy Calls, Clarity Calls, coaching)

You must give **at least 24 hours' notice** to cancel or reschedule a booked session. If you don't, you are not entitled to a refund or a free reschedule and you'll need to pay for another session.

Reschedule via the booking platform link in your confirmation email — see [How to reschedule or rebook a session](/help-center/account-and-billing/reschedule-or-rebook).

## Online courses and Clarity Calls — 14-day refund

If you'd like a refund for an online course or a Clarity Call you have purchased, you must notify AdvanceMed **within 14 days of purchase**. After 14 days, or if the purchase is part of a package, the 14-day refund doesn't apply.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions auto-renew. To stop the next renewal, cancel **before the renewal date** using the customer dashboard:

1. Log in at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au).
2. Go to your dashboard.
3. Click the plan you want to cancel and follow the prompts.

See [How to cancel or change your subscription](/help-center/account-and-billing/cancel-or-change-subscription) for the full walk-through.

## Events

For in-person or online events, you must give **at least 30 days' written notice** to be eligible for a refund. AdvanceMed may offer a recorded replay (which may carry an additional fee).

## Administration fee

Where a refund is provided at AdvanceMed's discretion, **a 10% administration fee** may be deducted to cover operational costs.

## Change-of-mind refunds

AdvanceMed generally does not offer change-of-mind refunds outside the windows above. Discretionary refunds or service credits may be offered case by case.

## Payments

All prices are in Australian Dollars. Payments are processed by third-party providers (Stripe and PayPal). Pricing errors are corrected by either offering you the correct price or cancelling and refunding the order.

## Need to talk about a refund?

Email **support@advancemed.com.au**. Include your order ID, the date of purchase, and what you'd like to happen.
