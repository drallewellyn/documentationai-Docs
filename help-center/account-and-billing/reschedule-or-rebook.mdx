## Quick answer

Use the **booking link in your confirmation email** to reschedule, cancel, or book another session. The same link works for all three actions.

If you've lost the link, the master booking page is:
[tidycal.com/advancemed/strategy-call-prepaid](https://tidycal.com/advancemed/strategy-call-prepaid)

<Callout kind="warning">
  You must reschedule or cancel **at least 24 hours before** your booked time. After that, you'll need to pay for a new session — see the [Refund policy](/help-center/account-and-billing/refund-and-cancellation).
</Callout>

## Step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your confirmation email">
    Look for the email titled **"Your AdvanceMed appointment is confirmed"**. The booking link is near the bottom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the booking link">
    The link opens your booking page on TidyCal — our scheduling platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the action">
    - **Reschedule** — select a new date / time.
    - **Cancel** — release the slot.
    - **Book another session** — choose any future slot in the same flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    You'll receive a fresh confirmation email and a text reminder the day before your new appointment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Booking another session for the future

Existing clients can book future sessions through the same link without going through checkout again, **provided the booking is paid for** (e.g. you're on an active coaching package). For one-off paid sessions like a Clarity Call, you'll need to purchase the new session before booking.

## Can I have someone else attend in my place?

Coaching is non-transferable — sessions are sold for one named client. If you'd like a colleague to engage, they should book separately.

## Trouble with the link

If the link is expired or you can't access TidyCal, email **support@advancemed.com.au** with your registered email and preferred new time. We'll either rebook on your behalf or send a fresh booking link.
