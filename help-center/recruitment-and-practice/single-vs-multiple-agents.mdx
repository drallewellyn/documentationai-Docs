If you're working as a **locum**, you are not obliged to sign with just one company.

In fact, it generally makes sense to **sign with more than one** initially, because no single agency normally has access to all the jobs that may be available.

By working with a range of agencies, you'll often find one or two that suit your needs better. Many doctors who locum for a while eventually settle on one particular agency as their preferred agent.
