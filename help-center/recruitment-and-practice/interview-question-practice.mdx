You'll generally find that if you look online and ask within your networks, you can get hold of some interview questions to prepare with.

## Vet what you find

It's important to **vet** the questions you collect:

- Make sure they're **relevant to the selection criteria** and other information about the selection process.
- Questions from **last year** are likely still based on the same process.
- Questions from **5 years ago** are probably not.

## AdvanceMed Question Bank

To help you get started, we have collected **over 900 questions** from a range of medical job interview types in the [AdvanceMed Question Bank](https://questionbank.advancemed.com.au/).
