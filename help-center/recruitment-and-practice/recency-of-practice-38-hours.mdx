---
title: Can extra hours over 38 in a week count towards recency of practice?
description: No — only the first 38 hours in any given week count toward recency-of-practice requirements.
tags: [career-pathway]
source_url: https://help.advancemed.com.au/article/45-if-i-work-more-than-38-hours-in-a-week-can-i-count-these-additional-hours-towards-demonstrating-recency-of-practice
last_migrated: 2026-05-09
---

**No.**

You may only accumulate **38 hours in one week**. Additional time will not count towards the standard.

This means that regardless of how many hours you work beyond the 38-hour threshold in a single week, only the first 38 hours can be counted toward demonstrating recency-of-practice requirements.
