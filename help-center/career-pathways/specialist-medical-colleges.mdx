For IMGs already qualified as specialists overseas, the relevant college performs your **comparability assessment** under the [Specialist Pathway](/help-center/international-medical-graduates/work-as-specialist-doctor). For doctors planning to enter Australian specialty training, the college runs the relevant training program and selection process.

## Three things to know about colleges

1. **Each college runs its own selection and assessment** — there is no central appeals process across colleges.
2. **Costs and timelines differ enormously** — application fees, training fees, and exam costs vary by college, often by tens of thousands of dollars.
3. **Some specialties have multiple sub-faculties under one parent college** — for example, RACP umbrellas more than a dozen sub-specialties under "Adult Medicine" and "Paediatrics".
