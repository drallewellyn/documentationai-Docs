AdvanceMed is **the Career Doctors** — a medical career service based in Newcastle, NSW, Australia, supporting doctors at every stage of their training and career, online across Australia and New Zealand.

For the latest team and statistics, see [advancemed.com.au/about](https://advancemed.com.au/about/).

## Dr Anthony Llewellyn — Managing Director

**Credentials:** BMedSci, MBBS, FRANZCP, MHA, GAICD, Cert Coaching

Anthony is a Consultant Psychiatrist with **20+ years' experience** in medical leadership, training, and recruitment. Highlights from his background include:

- **4 years as Medical Director** of the Health Education & Training Institute (HETI) in NSW
- **Education Specialist** with the Royal Australian and New Zealand College of Psychiatrists (RANZCP)
- Former **Adjunct Associate Professor**, University of Newcastle School of Medicine and Public Health
- **Chaired and conducted 1,000+ job interviews** in medical recruitment panels
- Currently helps **~1,000 doctors a year** through coaching, courses, and document services
