title: How do I access my customer dashboard?
description: AdvanceMed has two customer portals — coaching.advancemed.com.au for most clients, and doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au for VIP members.

AdvanceMed has **two customer portals**. Which one you use depends on what you've purchased.

## Most clients — `coaching.advancemed.com.au`

If you've bought a Strategy / Clarity Call, a coaching session, a course, document services, or a subscription, this is your portal.

**Direct dashboard link:** [coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard/)

What's there:

- **Invoices and payment history**
- **Course access** for any courses you've enrolled in
- **Document service intake forms** at [coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/forms/)
- **Booking calendars** for upcoming sessions
- **Subscriptions / Plans** — manage cancellations, upgrades, downgrades
- **Payment methods** — add or remove credit cards (handled securely by Stripe)

## VIP members — `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au`

If you're on a **VIP program** (General VIP, SET VIP, or Mini VIP — including Anthony and Melody Koo's programs), this is your portal.

**Login URL:** [doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au)

What's there:

- **Support forums** for VIP members
- **Coaching links** for booked sessions
- **VIP courses** including specialty-specific content
- **AI tools** available only to VIP members

For an overview of the VIP program and what's included in each tier, see [VIP portal](/help-center/services/vip-portal).

## Common questions

**I never created an account.** If you bought a service, an account was created automatically using your purchase email. See [I can't reset my password](/help-center/troubleshooting/cannot-reset-password) for how to set one up.

**Which portal applies to me?** If you've ever purchased a VIP program (General, SET, or Mini), use `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au`. For all other AdvanceMed purchases (Strategy Calls, CV rewrites, individual courses, subscriptions), use `coaching.advancemed.com.au`.

**My email looks wrong on the system.** Email **support@advancemed.com.au** with both your purchase email and the email you'd like to use. We can reassign your account.

**I can't find a session recording.** See [Session recordings](/help-center/account-and-billing/session-recordings).

> **Note:** `my.advancemed.com.au` is the old site and is being phased out. New purchases and account access happen at `coaching.advancemed.com.au` (or `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au` for VIP members).
