Your customer dashboard lives at **[coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au)**.

## Step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to coaching.advancemed.com.au" icon="globe">
    Bookmark this URL for quick access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in" icon="log-in">
    Enter the email and password you used at purchase. Use **Forgot Password** if you've lost your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your dashboard" icon="layout">
    You'll usually be taken straight to your dashboard. If not, click your icon at the top right and select **Dashboard** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's on the dashboard

- **Subscriptions / Plans** — manage cancellations, upgrades, downgrades
- **Bookings** — your past and upcoming sessions
- **Recordings** — links to your session recordings
- **Document deliverables** — your CV / resume PDFs
- **Payment methods** — add or remove credit cards (handled by Stripe)

## Common questions

**I never created an account.** If you bought a service, an account was created automatically using your purchase email. Use **Forgot Password** to set one.

**My email looks wrong.** Email **support@advancemed.com.au** with both your purchase email and the email you'd like to use. We can reassign your account.

**I can't find a recording.** See [Session recordings](/help-center/account-and-billing/session-recordings).
