## Overview

To access your Advancemed dashboard:

1. Go to `https://dashboard.advancemed.com`
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Login"

If you're new:

- Click "Sign Up" to create an account
- Follow email verification steps

Forgot password?

- Use "Forgot Password" to reset via email
