title: I can't reset my password
description: Common reasons password reset emails don't arrive, and what to do next.
---

Most password-reset issues are one of three things — work through them in order.

## Quick fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your spam / junk folder" icon="alert-triangle">
    Reset emails come from a `noreply@` address and are commonly filtered. Search for "advancemed" in your mail app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the email address" icon="mail">
    Make sure you're requesting the reset using the **email you used at purchase**. If you have more than one email account, try them all.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request a fresh reset link" icon="refresh-cw">
    Reset links expire. Go to [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au), click **Forgot Password**, and request a new one. Use it within 30 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Still not working?

Email **support@advancemed.com.au** with:

- Your registered email
- The approximate date you bought your service (so we can locate the account)
- A note saying you've already checked spam and tried a fresh reset

We'll either send a manual reset link or move your account to a different email if needed.

## What we can't do

- Reset your password over chat or phone — only via email so we can verify you control the registered address
- Send your existing password — passwords are hashed and we can't read them
