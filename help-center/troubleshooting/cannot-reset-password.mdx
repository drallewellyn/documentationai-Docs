---
title: I can't reset my password
description: Password reset for both AdvanceMed customer portals — coaching.advancemed.com.au and doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au — plus what to do if the reset email doesn't arrive.
---

AdvanceMed has two customer portals. Reset your password from the one that matches your account — see [How do I access my customer dashboard?](/help-center/faq/how-to-access-dashboard) if you're not sure which.

## Most clients — `coaching.advancemed.com.au`

You can reset your password **or request a one-time email login** from the customer dashboard sign-in page:

[coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/customer-dashboard/)

The email login option is useful if you've forgotten your password and just want to get in once.

## VIP members — `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au`

VIP members reset their password at:

[doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au/forgot-password](https://doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au/forgot-password/)

## Quick fix (works for both portals)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your spam / junk folder" icon="alert-triangle">
    Reset emails come from a `noreply@` or AdvanceMed system address and are commonly filtered. Search for "advancemed" in your mail app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the email address" icon="mail">
    Make sure you're requesting the reset using the **email you used at purchase**. If you have more than one email account, try them all.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request a fresh reset link" icon="refresh-cw">
    Reset links expire. Go back to the relevant reset URL above and request a new one. Use it within 30 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try the email login option (coaching portal only)" icon="log-in">
    If reset emails still don't arrive, request an **email login link** from the coaching customer dashboard sign-in page — same end result, different mechanism.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Still not working?

Email **support@advancemed.com.au** with:

- Your registered email
- Which portal you're trying to access (`coaching.advancemed.com.au` or `doctorcoaching.advancemed.com.au`)
- The approximate date you bought your service (so we can locate the account)
- A note saying you've already checked spam and tried a fresh reset

We'll either send a manual reset link or move your account to a different email if needed. Same support inbox handles both portals.

## What we can't do

- Reset your password over chat or phone — only via email so we can verify you control the registered address
- Send your existing password — passwords are hashed and we can't read them
