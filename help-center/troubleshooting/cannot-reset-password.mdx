---
title: Cannot Reset Password
description: Troubleshoot issues resetting your Advancemed password.
---

## Symptoms

- No reset email received
- Reset link expired or not working

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check spam folder">
    Look for the reset email in Spam/Junk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request again">
    Go to `https://dashboard.advancemed.com/forgot-password` and repeat the process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issues continue, email support@advancemed.com.
  </Step>
</Steps>
