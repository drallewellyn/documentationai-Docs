The Australian internship system is largely **off-limits to anyone other than a graduate from an Australian (or New Zealand) medical school** — including international students who studied in Australia.

IMG doctors generally **cannot** apply for intern programs or internships in Australia.

## What you're probably looking for instead

In most cases, IMGs are not actually looking for an Internship — they're looking for a **Resident Medical Officer (RMO)** job under the [Standard Pathway](/help-center/international-medical-graduates/amc-exam-exemptions).
