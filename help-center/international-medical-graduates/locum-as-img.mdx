---
title: Can I work as a locum as an International Medical Graduate?
description: It's essentially impossible to work locums as an IMG during your initial registration period — supervision and visa rules get in the way.
tags: [imgs, recruitment]
source_url: https://help.advancemed.com.au/article/41-can-i-work-locums-as-an-international-medical-graduate
last_migrated: 2026-05-09
---

It is essentially impossible to work as a locum as an IMG because of the **requirement to be supervised** during your initial registration period.

After you have obtained **general or specialist registration**, you may then be able to do locums. However, you may still be limited because of **visa requirements**.
