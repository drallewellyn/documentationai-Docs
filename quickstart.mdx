## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Basic information about your medical career stage (e.g., residency, specialization interests)

</Callout>

Advancemed supports doctors at all career levels, from interns to consultants.

## Create Your Account

Setting up your Advancemed account takes just a few minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the Advancemed registration page at `https://advancemed.com.au/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your full name, professional email (e.g., `dr.smith@hospital.com`), and select your medical specialty from the dropdown.
    
    Choose a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Advancemed and click the confirmation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile Setup" icon="edit-3">
    Answer initial questions about your career goals, such as target roles or location preferences (e.g., Newcastle or online).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In to Your Dashboard

Once registered, log in securely from any device.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop Browser" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser, go to `https://advancemed.com.au/login`, and enter your email and password.
    
    Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security during your first login.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the Advancemed mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.
    
    Use the same credentials and scan the QR code for quick setup.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Run Your First Career Assessment

Experience Advancemed's core value immediately by completing an assessment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Assessment" icon="search">
    From the dashboard, click **Career Assessment** and choose **Quick Career Match**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer Questions" icon="help-circle">
    Respond to 10-15 multiple-choice questions about your experience, preferences, and goals (takes ~5 minutes).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results" icon="bar-chart-3">
    View your personalized report, including recommended roles, interview tips, and next steps.
    
    Save or share the PDF summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Assessments update dynamically as you add more profile details. Re-run them after coaching sessions for refined insights.
</Callout>
