## What happens after you purchase

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirmation email" icon="mail">
    You'll receive an email from AdvanceMed confirming your purchase. Keep it — most follow-up actions (booking link, intake form, dashboard access) live in there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intake or booking" icon="edit-3">
    Depending on the service:

    - **Sessions** → use the booking link in your confirmation email to pick a time. See [How to reschedule or rebook](/help-center/account-and-billing/reschedule-or-rebook).
    - **CV rewrite** → complete the intake form linked from your confirmation email and upload your current CV. See [How to submit your CV](/help-center/guides/submit-your-cv).
    - **Course** → log in at [coaching.advancemed.com.au](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au) and start at module 1.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delivery" icon="check-circle">
    - **Sessions** are recorded — see [Session recordings](/help-center/account-and-billing/session-recordings).
    - **CV rewrites** return a first draft within ~5 working days, with a video walkthrough.
    - **Courses** are immediately accessible after enrolment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Got a question before you buy?

The fastest way to get a question answered is the [support form on advancemed.com.au](https://advancemed.com.au/contact-us/) — pick the most relevant subject so it routes correctly.

If your question is "is X service right for me?" the right answer is often a [Clarity Call](/help-center/services/strategy-clarity-calls) — that's exactly what those are for.
