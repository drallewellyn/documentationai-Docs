## How the help center is organised

```
Help Center
├── Getting Started        — orientation, services overview, about us
├── Services               — Strategy Calls, CV rewrite, cover letters, pricing
├── International Medical Graduates  — pathways, exams, internships, visas
├── Career Pathways        — psychiatry, nursing, AHPRA, public vs private
├── Recruitment & Practice — agencies, CPD, interviews, recency of practice
└── Account & Billing      — subscriptions, recordings, support, privacy
```

## What this knowledge base is not

- Not a substitute for [terms](https://advancemed.com.au/terms/) or [privacy](https://advancemed.com.au/privacy/) policies — those legal documents take precedence.
- Not migration advice (Registered Migration Agents and Migration Lawyers only).
- Not specific clinical, financial, or legal advice.
