## Overview

Advancemed provides expert coaching to help doctors navigate their medical careers. You receive personalized guidance on job applications, interviews, and career progression. This guide covers booking sessions, preparation tips, format options, and follow-up resources.

<Callout kind="info">
  All coaching sessions focus on your unique challenges, whether you're an IMG seeking Australian opportunities or advancing in specialist training.
</Callout>

## Booking Your Coaching Session

Follow these steps to schedule your first session quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Booking Page" icon="external-link">
    Go to the [coaching booking page](https://coaching.advancemed.com.au/strategy-call/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Package" icon="package">
    Choose from 1-on-1 strategy calls, courses, or document services based on your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Form" icon="edit-3">
    Fill out the intake form with your career goals, current challenges, and preferred dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Pay" icon="credit-card">
    Review your selection, complete payment, and receive a confirmation email with session details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Preparing for Your Session

Maximize your time by preparing thoroughly.

- Review your CV and recent career milestones.
- List 3-5 specific questions, such as interview strategies or job market insights for IMGs.
- Test your video setup if online.

<Expandable title="Advanced Preparation Checklist" default-open="false">
  - Gather supporting documents like job applications or rejection feedback.
  - Research Advancemed's free resources, such as the [Interview Question Bank](https://questionbank.advancemed.com.au).
  - Set measurable goals, e.g., "Prepare for 5 common interview questions."
</Expandable>
