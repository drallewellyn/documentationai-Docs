## Recent Updates

Stay informed about the latest enhancements to Advancemed, empowering you with better career guidance tools for your medical journey.

<Update label="2024-11-15" description="v2.1.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added AI-powered interview simulator integrated with the free question bank.
- Introduced personalized career path recommendations based on your specialty and experience.

## Improvements

- Enhanced mobile responsiveness for coaching dashboards.
- Optimized search functionality in the IMG resources section.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed login issues for international medical graduates.
- Resolved document upload errors in coaching services.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-10-20" description="v2.0.0" tags={["feature", "breaking"]}>

## New Features

- Launched new vlog integration with career blog for video content playback.
- Added wall of love testimonials feed on the dashboard.

## Breaking Changes

- Updated authentication flow requiring two-factor setup for coaching access.
- Migrated legacy document services to new API endpoints.

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected free AI career tools accessibility for first-time users.
- Patched contact form submission delays.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-10" description="v1.9.0" tags={["bugfix", "improvement"]}>

## Improvements

- Streamlined strategy call booking process with calendar sync.
- Improved performance of courses listing with lazy loading.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed blog/vlog search indexing issues.
- Resolved payment gateway glitches for course enrollments.

</Update>

## How to Stay Updated

<Callout kind="tip">

Subscribe to our newsletter or enable notifications in your Advancemed dashboard at `https://dashboard.advancemed.com` to receive changelog alerts directly.

</Callout>

Follow these steps to check for updates manually:

<Steps>

<Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">

Access your account at `https://advancemed.com.au`.

</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Settings" icon="settings">

Go to your profile and select "Updates" tab.

</Step>

<Step title="Review Changelog" icon="book-open">

View release notes and apply any required actions.

</Step>

</Steps>
