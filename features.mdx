## What's not on offer

- **Migration advice** — only Registered Migration Agents and Migration Lawyers can give individual migration advice. See [Visa types](/help-center/international-medical-graduates/visa-types).
- **Specific clinical advice** — AdvanceMed is a career service, not a clinical training or supervision provider.
- **Specific financial or legal advice** — general information only.
